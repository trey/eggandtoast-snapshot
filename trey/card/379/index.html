Ground Turkey Breast Meatloaf From the kitchen of trey

  • 2# ground turkey breast
  • 3 medium slices soft bread, torn in pieces, and 1 cup milk or 1 cup dry bread crumbs and 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/4 c minced onion
  • 1 1/4 t salt (I don’t use this)
  • 1/4 t each pepper, dry mustard, sage, celery salt and garlic salt (or granules)
  • 1 T Worcestershire sauce or tamari soy sauce

Heat oven to 350ยบ (mod.). Mix all ingredients thoroughly. For better browning, shape into loaf on shallow baking pan.

Bake 1 1/2 hr., or until done. Serve hot or cold. For Catsup-topped Loaf, spread 3 T catsup on top before baking.

8 servings.

(1 serving = 3 Weight Watcher plus points)
