Easy Chocolate Pudding Pie From the kitchen of marc

  • 1 box jello instant chocolate pudding
  • 1 box jello instant vanilla pudding
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • cool whip
  • 1 chocolate bar
  • 1 8” graham cracker pie crust

Combine pudding, milk and ice cream with a hand mixer and pour into crust. Refrigerate until set, about an hour. Top with whipped cream. Using a carrot peeler and the chocolate bar, cover the top with chocolate shavings.
