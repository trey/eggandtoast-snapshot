Yummy Spinach From the kitchen of wyattdanger

Ingredients:

  • Bag of Spinach
  • Olive Oil
  • Chopped Garlic

Instructions:

Heat olive oil in pan over low heat. Add a TSP of garlic, heat until smell fills kitchen.

Add a half bag of spinach and stir around until desired consistency.
