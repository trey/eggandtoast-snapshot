PUMPKIN BREAD From the kitchen of Crigsy

I want to attempt making a Vegan version of this and posting that recipe down the line. For now, this recipe is amazing and you will love every bite.

1t each: ground cloves, cinnamon, nutmeg, allspice
3c sugar
1c oil
4 eggs
1 can of pumpkin
2/3c water
2 1/2c flour
1/2t. baking powder
1t salt
2t baking soda
 (won’t need baking powder, salt or soda if you use self-rising flour)

Mix together sugar, oil and eggs. Add pumpkin and water. Sift together and add dry ingredients and spices. Pour into two (regular sized) loaf pans lined with parchment paper. (cooking spray works, too). Cook at 350degrees for one hour (less for glass loaf pans). I normally don’t preheat, I just turn it to 350 right before I pour the mix in the loaf pan. Freezes well, if it lasts that long!

I’ve used it for the last 3+ years and I love it! :)
