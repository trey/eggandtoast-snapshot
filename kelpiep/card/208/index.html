Pesto Means Paste From the kitchen of kelpiep

Ingredients

  • A ton of Basil
  • Some nuts (pine, wal, cashew)
    • Toast ‘em up if you’re feeling fancy.
  • Parmesan cheese (at least ⅓ cup)
  • A few cloves of garlic
  • A bunch of olive oil

Instructions

  • Throw it in a food processor and keep adding things until it tastes how you want and is the consistency you want.
