Olivia’s MamMaw’s Sweet Potato Casserole From the kitchen of kelpiep

  • 3 c cooked sweet potatoes (can bake them, scoop out their insides, and puree them a little bit in a food processor)
  • 1 t vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 c. sugar
  • 1/2 c. melted butter

Topping:

  • 1/3 c. melted butter
  • 1 c. chopped pecans
  • 1/3 c. self-rising flour

Mix first 5 ingredients and put in a greased casserole dish.

Mix topping ingredients together and put on top of casserole.

Bake at 350ยบ for 20 min.
