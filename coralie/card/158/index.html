Molasses Cookies From the kitchen of coralie

Ingredients

  • 1/2c butter
  • 1c packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2c light molasses
  • 2 1/2c all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground ginger

Directions

  1. Cream together butter and brown sugar. Stir in egg and molasses and mix well.

  2. Fold in dry ingredients and stir.

  3. Cover and chill until firm (1-2hrs)

  4. Preheat oven to 350

  5. Roll dough into small balls and roll in white sugar. Place on lightly greased cookie sheet.

  6. Bake 7-10 min. Leave on cookie sheet 1 min until set.

  7. EAT!
