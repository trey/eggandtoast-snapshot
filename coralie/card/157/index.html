Zucchini Quiche From the kitchen of coralie

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs zucchini, grated and well drained
  • 4 medium eggs, beaten
  • 1.5c grated sharp cheddar
  • 1c grated swiss
  • 1c chopped red onion
  • 1/2c half & half
  • 1 Tbsp all purpose flour
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • pinch black pepper

Directions

  1. Squeeze excess moisture from zucchini

  2. Saute the onion in butter until wilted

  3. Mix the cheeses, zucchini and onion together

  4. Add remaining ingredients, mix thoroughly

  5. Pour into a lightly greased 9 in pie plate (no need for crust)

  6. Bake @ 350 for 30 min or until toothpick in middle comes out clean

  7. EAT!
