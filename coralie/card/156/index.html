Spinach Puff Pastry Quiche From the kitchen of coralie

Ingredients

  • 1 sheet frozen puff pastry (half of a 17.25oz package)
  • 3oz package cream cheese
  • 1/3 c half & half
  • 3 eggs
  • 10oz package frozen, chopped spinach, thawed & drained
  • 1/2c grated cheddar
  • 1/4c grated parmesan
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425

  2. Roll puff pastry to 11 in square & put in 9 in diameter pie plate. Trim edges.

  3. Beat cream cheese in medium bowl until smooth. Gradually beat in half & half and eggs. Mix in remaining ingredients.

  4. Pour mixture into prepared crust.

  5. Bake until crust golden brown and filling is set, aprox 25 min.

  6. Cool 10 min before serving (so you don’t burn your mouth!)
